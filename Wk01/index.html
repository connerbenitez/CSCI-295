Cheeseburger Recipe

Description

How to make THE BEST cheeseburger! Perfect burger patties every. single. time. Includes an epic burger sauce too!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 3 tbs dill pickle relish
  • 1 tbs dijon mustard
  • 2 lbs ground beef (80/20)
  • Kosher salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbs canola oil
  • 6 slices American cheese

Instructions

  1. BURGER SAUCE: In a small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, ketchup, dill pickle relish and Dijon; set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, combine beef, 1 1/2 teaspoons salt and 1 1/2 teaspoons pepper. Using a wooden spoon or clean
    hands, stir until well combined. Gently form into 6 1-inch-thick patties, about the size of the hamburger buns.
  3. Heat canola oil in a large cast iron skillet over medium high heat. Add patties and cook until lightly charred
    or until desired doneness, about 3-5 minutes per side; top with cheese.
  4. Serve immediately in hamburger buns with BURGER SAUCE and desired toppings
